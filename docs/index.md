<p class="section-label">The problem</p>
<h2 class="centered">Shopping rewards impatience. Your wallet pays for it.</h2>

You see it, you want it, you buy it — at today's price, plus shipping, plus
whatever you forgot to add up. Prices swing wildly across stores and across the
year, and India's biggest discounts arrive in predictable festive waves. Nobody
has time to check six apps every day for three months.

**Patiently does.** You declare the *intent*; we supply the *patience*.

<p class="section-label">How it works</p>
<h2 class="centered">Three steps. Then you forget about it.</h2>

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   <span class="step-num">1</span> __Add a wish__

    ---

    "Sony WH-1000XM5, under ₹26,990, within 4 months."

-   <span class="step-num">2</span> __We hunt, daily__

    ---
